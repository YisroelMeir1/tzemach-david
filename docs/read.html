Year 1 · The First Year of Creation

 Machine translation · Review pending
In the first year of Creation, the Holy One, blessed be He, created — out of absolute nothing — during the six days of Creation, the heavens and the earth and all their host; and on the sixth day of Creation, Adam, the first man, was formed.

Year 2448 · The Exodus from Egypt

 Machine translation · Review pending
AM 2448 — The children of Israel went out of Egypt at the end of 430 years. All the miracles were performed for them — the splitting of the Sea of Reeds, the war with Amalek, the descent of the manna, the giving of the Torah — the Tabernacle was built, and all the narratives of the book of Exodus: all of this was done in the first year after the Exodus. And all this was in the year AM 2448.
תמ״ח — יצאו בני ישראל ממצרים מקץ ת״ל שנה. נעשו להם כל הניסים — קריעת ים סוף, מלחמת עמלק, ירידת המן, מתן תורה — ונבנה המשכן, וכל סיפורי ספר שמות: הכל בשנה הראשונה ליציאת מצרים. והכל בשנת ב׳תמ״ח ליצירה.
